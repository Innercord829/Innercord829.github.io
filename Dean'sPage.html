Smolder Bio

Hidden amongst the craggy cliffs of the Noxian frontier, under the watchful eyes of his mother, a young dragon is learning what it means to be heir to the Camavoran imperial dragon lineage. Playful and eager to grow up, Smolder looks for any excuse to practice his burgeoning abilites. Though he's still a fledgling, his skills are nothing to sneeze at, easily setting fire to anything that burns. ..more

Smolder Abilites

Passive Ability

Dragon Practice

Hitting champions with Abilites and killing enemies with Super Scorcher Breath grants a stack of Dragon Practice. Stacks increase the damage of Smolders basic Abilites

Q Ability

Super Scorcher Breath

Smolder breathes fire on an enemy. As he gains more stacks, this ability becomes more powerful

W Ability

ACHOOO!

Smolder lets out an adorable glaming sneeze that explodes when hitting enemy champions

E Ability

Flap, Flap, Flap

Smolder takes flight ignoring terrain and attacking the lowest health enemy

R Ability

MMOOOMMMM!

Smolder calls his mom to breath fire from above, dealing extra damage and slowing enemies in the center of her fire
